# 🛒 Supermarket Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Supermarket Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze supermarket sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, branch-wise sales, and payment trends. The dashboard provides actionable business insights through interactive visualizations, helping management monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyze branch and city-wise sales.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Understand customer purchasing patterns.
- Track payment method preferences.
- Generate business insights for better decision-making.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Branch A recorded the highest sales contribution.
- Cash is the most preferred payment method.
- Soap generated the highest sales among all products.
- Rice was the lowest-performing product.
- Sales peaked in April and declined slightly afterward.
- Hyderabad Branch generated the highest overall sales.
- The business served 100 customers with 463 total orders.
